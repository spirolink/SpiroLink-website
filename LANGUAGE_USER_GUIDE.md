## FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Will my language choice be saved?
**A:** Yes! Your language preference is saved in your browser and will be remembered the next time you visit.

### Q: Does the website work in my language?
**A:** Yes! We support 12 languages. If your language isn't listed, contact us and we can add it.

### Q: Do I need to select the language every time?
**A:** No! Your selection is saved automatically. You only need to change it if you want a different language.

### Q: What if I clear my browser cookies/cache?
**A:** Your language preference will be reset to the default. Just select your language again.

### Q: Does changing language affect my account?
**A:** No! Changing language only affects the display of text. It doesn't impact any accounts or data.

### Q: Is the translation automatic?
**A:** No, our website is professionally translated into all 12 languages.

### Q: Can I suggest a translation correction?
**A:** Yes! Please contact us at hello@spirolink.com with the correction.

### Q: Why are some technical terms not translated?
**A:** Technical terms like GPON, DWDM, and FTTH are industry standards and remain consistent across all languages for clarity.

## Troubleshooting

### **Language selector not visible**
- Scroll to the top right of your browser
- Check if it's in the header navigation
- Try refreshing the page

### **Language not changing**
- Try clicking the language selector again
- Refresh the page after selecting language
- Check that your browser allows localStorage

### **Language reverts to English**
- Your browser might be clearing localStorage
- Check privacy settings and allow localStorage for this website

### **Text still showing in English**
- Clear your browser cache and refresh
- Try a different language first, then switch back
